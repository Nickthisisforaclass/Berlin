Berlin: A Week to Remember

A special trip with my twin sister

Why Berlin?

Last year, my twin sister and I spent an unforgettable week in Berlin. It was one of the best trips we've ever taken together. From the history to the culture and food, every day was full of excitement and discovery.

Favorite Memories

  • Exploring the Berlin Wall and East Side Gallery
  • Eating döner kebab from a street cart at midnight
  • Laughing at how lost we got in the U-Bahn
  • Visiting Museum Island and pretending to be art critics
  • Watching the sunset from the top of the Berlin TV Tower

Why I Love It

Berlin has this amazing balance between rich history and modern creativity. I loved how every neighborhood felt different. But more than anything, I loved sharing it all with my sister — it’s a trip that brought us even closer.
